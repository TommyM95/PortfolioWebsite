Head Escape

Head Escape is a roguelite/bullet hell game. In this game, players take control of their inner Avatar and embark on a journey to clear their mind of anger, sadness and anxiety manifestations. The game features procedurally generated rooms filled with loot, monsters and secrets to uncover. Players start with a basic weapon and no abilities, but can acquire loot and currency to upgrade their stats and unlock new abilities. The game focuses on providing an enjoyable player experience with varied gameplay options and a balance of challenge and progression.
